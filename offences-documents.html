Offences Relating to Documents

Document Laws in BNS:

Chapter XIX of the BNS modernizes the laws on Forgery and Electronic Records. Notably, the possession of forged documents and seals (previously IPC 473 & 474) has been split and remapped to BNS 337 and BNS 341. The definitions now explicitly include digital and electronic signatures.
