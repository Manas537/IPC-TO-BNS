Offences Relating to Marriage

Modernizing Matrimonial Law:

The BNS has significantly updated family-related penal laws. Most notably, IPC 497 (Adultery) has been omitted entirely following Supreme Court mandates. However, a new Section 69 now criminalizes sexual intercourse through deceitful means or a false promise of marriage. Cruelty (498A) remains a major focus under BNS 85.
