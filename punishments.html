Punishments under BNS

What has Changed in Penalties?

The BNS retains the classic punishments like Death, Imprisonment for Life, and Fines, but introduces Community Service (Section 4) as a revolutionary alternative for petty offences. It also clarifies that 'Life Imprisonment' means imprisonment for the remainder of a person's natural life.
